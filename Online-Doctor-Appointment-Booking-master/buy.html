Fever

Symptoms:

  • Elevated body temperature
  • Chills
  • Sweating
  • Headache
  • Muscle aches

Medications:

  • Acetaminophen: 500 mg tablets
  • Ibuprofen: 200 mg tablets
  • Aspirin: 325 mg tablets
  • Paracetamol: 500 mg tablets

Cough

Symptoms:

  • Persistent coughing
  • Chest tightness
  • Mucus production
  • Sore throat
  • Shortness of breath

Medications:

  • Dextromethorphan: 10 mg per 5 ml syrup
  • Guaifenesin: 100 mg tablets
  • Codeine: 10 mg per 5 ml syrup (for severe cough)
  • Honey: used in home remedies

Headache

Symptoms:

  • Pain or pressure in the head
  • Sensitivity to light or sound
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Dizziness
  • Tension or throbbing

Medications:

  • Acetaminophen: 500 mg tablets
  • Ibuprofen: 200 mg tablets
  • Naproxen: 250 mg tablets
  • Excedrin (contains acetaminophen, aspirin, and caffeine)

Cold

Symptoms:

  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Sneezing
  • Sore throat
  • Cough
  • Mild fever
  • Fatigue

Medications:

  • Pseudoephedrine: 30 mg tablets (decongestant)
  • Diphenhydramine: 25 mg tablets (antihistamine)
  • Phenylephrine: 10 mg tablets (decongestant)
  • Chlorpheniramine: 4 mg tablets (antihistamine)

Sore Throat

Symptoms:

  • Pain or scratchiness in the throat
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Redness or swelling in the throat
  • Dry throat
  • Hoarseness

Medications:

  • Benzocaine: 15 mg throat lozenges
  • Salt: for saline gargle
  • Hydrocodone: 5 mg per 5 ml syrup (for severe pain)
  • Lozenges with menthol: for soothing relief

Stomach Ache

Symptoms:

  • Abdominal pain or cramping
  • Bloating
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea or constipation

Medications:

  • Loperamide: 2 mg capsules (for diarrhea)
  • Antacids: (e.g., calcium carbonate 500 mg tablets)
  • Simethicone: 125 mg capsules (for gas relief)
  • Peppermint oil: used in capsules for digestive relief
